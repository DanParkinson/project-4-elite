About Us

Our history

Elite has been serving fine Indian cuisine since its inception in 1996. Our journey began with a passion for authentic flavors and a commitment to quality.

Our Founder

Safiyah Munshi, the visionary behind Elite, has dedicated their life to perfecting the art of Indian cooking. Their experience and love for food is evident in every dish we serve.

Our community

At Elite, we believe in giving back to our community. We regularly participate in free to use gardens with herbs and vegetables, and strive to make a positive impact.
